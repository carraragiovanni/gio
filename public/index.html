the Here Times

There's no lack of news, as a matter of fact there's an abundance thereof. Global news publishers have developed a cunning skill to inject sensational news into our screens, and have in consequence negatively affected the outreach of local news sources. The Here Times is here to change this, The Here Times is designed help you stay informed about what's happening on main street not on Wall Street.

The Here Times finds, curates, and beautifully presents articles from local and global news sources on a map right around you. Feel free to go read about what's happening in your backyard, where you have a loved one living, or where you're going on holiday.
